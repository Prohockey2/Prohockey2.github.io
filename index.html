Drills

This Paragraph will have different drills for different aspects

Handles

For this drill you neeed a few cones and a puck. You are going to lay the cones down in a straight line allowing just enough space for a puck and 1/3 to go through. You are going glide forawrd with your feet on the sides of the cones while stickhandling through them. Once done, do the same gliding backwards.

Speed

For this drill you will need a speed parachute. It's really simple, you're going to put on the parachute and skate as fast as you can to the other side, on the way back stop at both blue lines and go.
